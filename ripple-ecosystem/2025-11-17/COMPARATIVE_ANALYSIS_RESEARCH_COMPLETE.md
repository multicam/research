## Key Research Findings Summary

### Finding 1: Platform Specialization
XRP, Stellar, and Algorand occupy complementary market positions:
- **XRP:** Institutional payment bridge (proven, regulated, banking-focused)
- **Stellar:** Financial inclusion platform (merchant networks, emerging markets)
- **Algorand:** Enterprise infrastructure (scalability, smart contracts, DeFi)

### Finding 2: Technology Differentiation
Distinct consensus and architecture approaches:
- **XRP:** RPCA (deterministic, 1,500 TPS, 3-5s finality)
- **Stellar:** SCP (federated, 1,000+ TPS, 3-5s finality)
- **Algorand:** PPoS (randomized, 6,000+ TPS, instant finality)

### Finding 3: Adoption Metrics Divergence
Different activity patterns reflect positioning:
- **XRP:** 32K daily addresses, $2.91B 24h volume, 300+ banking partners
- **Stellar:** 73K daily addresses, $100-150M 24h volume, 20M merchant reach
- **Algorand:** 17-54K daily addresses, $50-90M 24h volume, enterprise focus

### Finding 4: Regulatory Clarity Variation
Three distinct regulatory paths:
- **XRP:** Highest US clarity (post-2025 SEC settlement, ETF approved)
- **Stellar:** Proactive compliance, no litigation
- **Algorand:** Building compliance infrastructure, addressing SEC concerns

### Finding 5: Deployment Success Stories
Real-world deployments demonstrate viability:
- **XRP:** ODL corridors active in 40+ routes (US-Mexico, Japan-Philippines, etc.)
- **Stellar:** 20M merchants in SE Asia (USDC/XLM), global remittance network
- **Algorand:** Marshall Islands CBDC, Bank of Italy projects, Google Cloud partnership
