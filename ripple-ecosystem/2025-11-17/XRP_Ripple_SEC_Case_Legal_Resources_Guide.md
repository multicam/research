## 2. COURT FILING TRACKING SERVICES

### Official Government Sources

**PACER (Public Access to Court Electronic Records)**
- **URL:** https://pacer.uscourts.gov/
- **Coverage:** All federal courts (district, bankruptcy, appellate, special courts)
- **Scope:** Over 1 billion documents with real-time updates
- **Features:** National case locator, court-specific searches
- **PACER Case Locator:** https://pcl.uscourts.gov/
- **Cost:** $0.10 per page; fees waived when quarterly usage â‰¤ $30
- **Use Case:** Direct access to all Ripple v. SEC filings, briefs, and court orders
- **Authority Level:** Highest - Official federal court records

### Third-Party Court Filing Services (Freemium & Premium)

**PacerPro**
- **URL:** https://www.pacerpro.com/
- **Focus:** Workflow automation for federal court filings
- **Features:** Case monitoring, automated alerts
- **User Base:** Major law firms trust this platform
- **Authority Level:** High - Industry-leading PACER automation tool

**CourtListener & RECAP Archive**
- **URL:** https://www.courtlistener.com/recap/
- **Coverage:** Federal district, bankruptcy, appellate, and special courts
- **Features:**
  - Searchable collection of millions of PACER documents
  - Free docket tracking (15 free tracks with browser extension)
  - 900,000+ hours of oral argument recordings

- **RECAP Extension:** Automatically adds documents to free archive
- **Cost:** Free basic; optional premium features
- **Publisher:** Free Law Project (501(c)(3) nonprofit)
- **Authority Level:** High - Nonprofit with academic backing (Princeton, Harvard)

**CourtAlert**
- **Coverage:** Federal District and Bankruptcy Courts, all 12 Circuit Courts, Federal Circuit, special courts
- **Features:** Case monitoring with customizable notification frequency
- **Authority Level:** Medium-High - Specialized court tracking service
