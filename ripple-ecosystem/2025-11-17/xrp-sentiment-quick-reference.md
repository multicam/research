## COMMUNITY HEALTH INDICATORS

### Green Flags ✅
- Consistent developer commits across multiple contributors
- Social volume increasing with positive sentiment
- Active governance discussions
- Regular technical proposals
- Strong on-chain activity

### Red Flags ⚠️
- Declining developer activity
- Negative sentiment trend
- Low governance participation
- Lack of technical discussions
- Decreasing on-chain activity
