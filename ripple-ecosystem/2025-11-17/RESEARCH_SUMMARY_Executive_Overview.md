### 2. COURT FILING TRACKING SERVICES

**Official Government Source:**
- **PACER** (pacer.uscourts.gov) - Official federal court records, all courts, $0.10 per page

**Premium Third-Party Services:**
- **PacerPro** - Market-leading workflow automation tool trusted by major law firms
- **CourtAlert** - Case monitoring with customizable alerts

**Free Alternatives:**
- **CourtListener RECAP** - Free searchable archive of millions of PACER documents
  - 15 free docket tracks with browser extension
  - Nonprofit operated by Free Law Project (Princeton/Harvard backed)
  - Growing collection of federal court records
  - **Recommended as best free option**

**University Resources:**
- Harvard Law, Stanford Law, University of Michigan Law Library all provide free docket research guides

**Key Finding:** CourtListener RECAP provides excellent free alternative to paid services; recommended starting point for court document tracking.

### 5. TIMELINE TRACKING RESOURCES

| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| December 22, 2020 | SEC files lawsuit against Ripple Labs |
| July 13, 2023 | Judge Analisa Torres ruling: XRP on exchanges NOT securities; institutional sales ($728M) violated securities laws |
